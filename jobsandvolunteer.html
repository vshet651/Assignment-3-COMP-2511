My Volunteer Experience

Hindu Temple Of Calgary 2017-2019

I was volunteering at my local temple when I was in grade 6 to grade 8. Where on the weekends, I would serve people food and clean up the temple and food hall. There would be around 100 to 150 people that would come every Saturday and Sunday for the food and to pray at the temple. Once my high school started I learned I couldn't hold down two volunteering gigs so I had to decide to cut this one.

Kumon 2018-2020

I was a student at Kumon, which was a learning center to help me improve my math and Language Arts skills. Here after I would finish my own work, I would help the staff by marking other students' books, helping them or be organizing paperwork in the back. These skills I learned here of time management, organization, and independent working helped me get my future jobs. Once the Covid-19 pandemic hit I was at the point of finishing the program and I did in August 2020. Because I didn't have transportation to get there the volunteering had to stop, and with the Covid-19 Pandemic they didn't many people volunteering.

My Job Experience

Subway July 2021-September 2021

This was my first-ever job, as the Subway I was working at was my uncle's. It was only a summer job, as I didn't want to sit at home and do nothing in the summer, instead I worked with him, learning how to organize stock, order stock, create great customer experiences, learn how to do tasks efficiently and many more things. Once my grade 12 year started in high school, I wanted to focus on school so I decided I didn't want to work while in school.

Best Buy October 2022-Current

This was my first job that I have kept long term. I have learned a lot from this job, as I have worked in many departments in the store I work at. But my main position is in the warehouse, where I do stock counts, break and put down our truck, fulfill online orders, while helping customers with their questions on the floor. I have made many of my closest friendships at this job and learned some important skills that I will take forward, to my future jobs once I graduate with my degree from Mount Royal University.
